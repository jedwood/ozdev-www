The GREENPRINT Properties

A series of modern and efficiently-scaled apartments in the heart of Salt Lake City offering attractive rents and ready-access to public transit. Their unique design maximizes rent-per-square-foot and supplies a growing market.
@ the Trax
The first micro apartment in Utah, GREENPRINT @ the Trax was completed in 2017. It has 60 units which leased within 4 months of completion and maintains a 97% occupancy rate. GREENPRINT @ the Trax is contiguous to the 900 South 200 West TRAX station.
West Temple
GREENPRINT West Temple is an expansion of the concept initiated by GREENPRINT @ the Trax with three buildings and 145 total micro units. Also conveniently located near a TRAX station, GREENPRINT West Temple will have a staged market entry beginning in Q1 2020.
Fairpark
GREENPRINT Fairpark will be located along North Temple within the historic Fairpark district and contains 155 apartments offering a varied unit mix. Ground breaking is planned for early 2020.
9th Station
Scheduled for 2020. Currently in the architectural design phase.
